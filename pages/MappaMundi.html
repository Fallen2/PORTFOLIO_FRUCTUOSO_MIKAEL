La Mappa Mundi d'Albi

Visualize the world as it was perceived in the 8th century in this 2D Puzzle Game

Assemble a puzzle of the Mappa Mundi and gather information about regions and population habits

"La Mappa Mundi d'Albi" is a serious game I developed during a 4-months internship at TECH’N’SMILE, a company specialised in Unity VR/AR application development. The project was playable on a tacticle table at " La Médiathèque d'Albi Pierre-Amalric" in 2019.
