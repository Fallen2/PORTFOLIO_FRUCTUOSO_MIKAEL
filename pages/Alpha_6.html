Alpha_6

A speedrun oriented FPS where time slow down when you aim

Remember the location of your foes and optimize your path to leave this unknown complex as fast as you can.

Alpha_6 is a first-person shooter game prototype developed as part of an university course. The development team is composed of 2 game developers.
In the project, I helded the positions of Gameplay Programmer and Game Designer.

Our goal was to provide a speedrun experience based on a simple mechanic. We came up with a mechanic based on bullets that bounce on walls and deal damages according to the number of bounces made before touching an enemy. With this concept, we could easily imagine speedrunners remembering every enemy spot and killing them without even looking at them.
