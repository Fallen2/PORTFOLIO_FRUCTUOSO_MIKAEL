My Name is Jiji

A cute point & click jam game

Discover the past of this lonely fisherman

My name is Jiji is a point & click short game made during a 48h gamejam. That was one of my first game jams and we challenged ourselves by using Game Maker 2.
In the project, I helded the positions of Gameplay Programmer.
