About me

Hey ! I’m Mikaël, a game design student who wants to become a Programmer/GameDesigner in the video game Industry. Thanks to my education, professional experience and personal projects, I developed versatile skills that made me work efficiently with people coming from all fields.

I enjoy fighting hard opponents with some friends on Monster Hunter and LoL ,get lost in the tremendous world of Metroidvania games like Dust and Hollow Knight, establish plans in turn based strategy games such as Into the Breach and Slay the Spire and immerse myself in the fascinating universes of Grim Fandango and Bioshock.

Looking forward to share my passion with you !
