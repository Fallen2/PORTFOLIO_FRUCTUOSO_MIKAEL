Hi! I’m Mikaël

I'm currently working as a Junior Gameplay Programmer at Appeal Studios on the game "Outcast: A New Beginning".

My passion for games has been a constant driving force throughout my career. I've actively participated in shaping the game design aspects of the projects I've been a part of, always seeking to understand the inner workings of games and why people are drawn to them. I’m thrilled when it comes to breaking down game rules, dissecting player preferences, and integrating insights from diverse fields to craft compelling game experiences.

Outside of work, you’ll find me delving into anime of all genres, taking part in (board) game activities or watching competitive sport contents and events.I also thrive on the opportunity to learn from others and share moments with friends and fellow enthusiasts, whether at home or in exciting outdoor settings.

Maybe our paths will cross in the near future and we’ll have the opportunity to connect and collaborate !
