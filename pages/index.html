About me

Hey ! I’m Mikaël, a student in video game management looking for a 6 months end-of-studies intership as soon as possible at your convenience.

I’ve always liked to deconstruct the rules of the games I play, breaking them down to their smallest component parts allowing me better understandings of what a game is made off, and often leading me to prototype small games.
What I like about being a gameplay programmer is that I can collaborate with creative people on the game design processes while developing mechanics and systems to deliver the funniest experience possible for players.
Today I aim for a gameplay programmer position in a studio where I will enrich my skills alongside inspiring people and create games that put players in the center of the game experience.

Looking forward to work with you.
