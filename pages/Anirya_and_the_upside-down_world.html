Anirya and the upside-down world

A 2D Metroidvania vertical slice in a fantasy world influenced by the Berber mythology

In this adventure, play as Anirya followed by her fire spirit companion Alim and try to reach the upside-down world to discover the secrets within

  • Explore both the human and the upside-down world accompanied by your friend Alim
  • Evolve with powerful new skills and abilities to fight creatures from the Berber mythology
  • Follow the development of Anirya throughout her personal journey

Anirya is a 2D metroidvania with an emphasis on narrative, rooted in Berber mythology. It highlights the themes of exclusion and the quest for self through Anirya's epic adventure in both the human and inverted worlds.
In the project, I held the positions of Gameplay Programmer, Game Designer and Team facilitator.
