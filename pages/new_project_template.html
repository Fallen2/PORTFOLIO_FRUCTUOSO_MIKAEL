Jurassic Snap

A VR Rail Shooter about snapping Dinos

Provoke dinosaurs, take your best shots and bring them back to the professor to learn more about these creatures !

Jurassic Snap is a VR Rail shooter about taking snapshots of dinosaurs. The game was developed by a 4 people team during my internship at Dreamirl Studio and got kickstarted in 2022.
In the project, I helded the positions of Gameplay Programmer and Team Facilitator. I was also involved in the game design process.
