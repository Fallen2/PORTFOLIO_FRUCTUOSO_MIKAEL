Jurassic Snap

A VR Rail Shooter about snapping Dinos

Provoke dinosaurs, take your best shots and bring them back to the professor to learn more about these creatures !

Jurassic Snap is a VR Rail shooter about taking snapshots of dinosaurs. The game was developed during my internship at Dreamirl Studio and got kickstarted in 2022.

  • Embark on a scientific mission to catalogue all the dinosaurs that live on this exoplanet.
  • Photograph and interact with the fauna to take the best pictures and discover all their behaviors.
  • Unveil the mysteries of this exoplanet during your expeditions

In the project, I helded the positions of Gameplay Programmer and Team Facilitator. I was also involved in the game design process.
