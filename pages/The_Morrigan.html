The Morrígan

A light RPG Vertical Slice in a world inspired by the Celtic mythology

In the Land of Éire, a people of druids seeking for knowledge settled in the Danann Forest, a place at the edge between the World of Men and the Sídh, the God’s Kingdom. In their research, Druids created occult masks bearing the image of Cernunnos and were able to use his powers. But these new powers were not legitimately obtained and Cernunnos cursed the usurpers. For their salvation, the tribes should send to the Danann Forest girls born on the Day of Samhain as an offering to the Morrígan.

The Morrígan is a light RPG Vertical Slice in which the player embodies a Young Celt willing to sacrifice in order to save her people. In the game, players will explore the dangerous Danann Forest and fight against the abominations of Cernunnos living there.

  • Explore the Dannan forest and search for answers regarding the curse of your tribe.
  • Deliver precise blows and develop strategies during your fights against the Cernunnos abomations to overcome the corruption.
  • As you get deeper in the mystic forest, become more powerful by accepting double-edged curses.

In the project, I held the positions of Gameplay Programmer and Scrum Master. I was also involved in the game design process.
