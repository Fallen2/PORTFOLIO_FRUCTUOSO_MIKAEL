Présentation

Anirya and the upside down world is a 2D metroidvania student game made in Unity. The team was composed of 10 students.

Unity 2D

Dev

Metroidvania 2D

Game Design

I participated in the game design research and worked on player skills, enemy designs and player progression features based on my metroidvania experience. I also worked on game balance and gamefeel issues.

Level Design

I level designed the first chapter of the game and its assets integration. The main challenge was to make a level design allowing the player to have a metroidvania experience in a 15-minute vertical slice.

Development

During the project, I was the Lead Developer and managed the development team. I worked on the following features:

  • Custom 2D physic player controller (movements, slope management prototype, combat)
  • Player and enemies interactions
  • Player related user interfaces
  • Respawn and checkpoint system
  • Wolf melee enemy AI
  • Waghzen (Boss) AI

Visual Effects

I participated in the animation creation process as the Lead Animator. I designed and implemented visual effects using Unity particle systems.
